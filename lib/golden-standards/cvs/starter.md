## WHY THIS CV IS GOLDEN STANDARD

1. **Education section is first and detailed**: For career starters, education IS the main qualification — GPA, coursework, TA role, and capstone all demonstrate competence
2. **Internship bullets are achievement-focused, not duty-focused**: "Developed automated testing framework reducing QA time by 35%" not "Assisted with testing"
3. **Projects substitute for missing work experience**: Each project has clear scope, tech stack, and measurable outcome (users, stars, adoption)
4. **Quantified everything possible**: 500+ users, 35% time reduction, 10K+ requests/second, 2M+ tweets — numbers make entry-level work feel substantial
5. **Technical skills section is comprehensive but honest**: Lists real technologies used in projects and internship, not aspirational skills
6. **Transferable skills shown through leadership**: Organizing hackathons, teaching, mentoring — signals collaboration ability
7. **Open source contributions demonstrate initiative**: Projects with GitHub stars and community adoption show ability to ship beyond classroom
8. **ATS-friendly format**: Single column, standard headers, clean formatting, keyword-rich skills section
9. **One page**: Appropriate for 0-2 years experience, no padding or filler content
