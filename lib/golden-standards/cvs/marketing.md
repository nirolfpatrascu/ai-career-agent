2. **Budget ownership stated explicitly**: $2.5M annual budget demonstrates trust and seniority
3. **Team leadership quantified**: Team of 8 + 3 agencies, mentored 4 specialists — shows management capability
4. **Growth metrics with baselines**: "12K to 85K monthly sessions" is more credible than "increased traffic 608%"
5. **Tool proficiency matches job postings**: HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Analytics, Semrush — high-frequency ATS keywords
6. **Career progression clear**: Coordinator → Manager → Senior Manager with expanding scope
7. **Modern marketing skills**: AI-driven personalization, ABM, marketing automation, content strategy — not outdated tactics
8. **Thought leadership**: Speaking engagements + publications signal industry authority
