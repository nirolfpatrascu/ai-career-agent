5. **Sales methodology fluency**: MEDDPICC, SPIN, Challenger, Sandler — signals coachability and process discipline
6. **Tool proficiency matches modern sales stack**: Salesforce, Gong, Outreach, Sales Navigator, Clari, ZoomInfo
7. **Career progression is clear**: BDR → AE Mid-Market → Senior AE Enterprise with increasing deal complexity and revenue targets
8. **Awards section provides social proof**: President's Club is universally understood as top-tier performance in sales
